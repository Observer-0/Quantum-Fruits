What's the goal?

We aim to reconcile general relativity and quantum mechanics using only what is already known‚Äîno speculative fields or extra dimensions. The emphasis is not on making new predictions but on showing how existing puzzles and data might look when you insist on strict unit bookkeeping and a quantized spacetime cell. These pages document the methodology and highlight open questions; the simulations let you play with the consequences.

"Reality defines itself through negation."

Information is not born from the outcome itself, but from the brutal exclusion of all alternatives that failed to manifest.

The Axiom of Realization

S_cell = 1

Working postulate: one discrete action unit per fundamental spacetime cell in this framework. This is a model choice, not an experimentally established law.

Instead of adding free dimensions, this approach prioritizes a minimal 4D description with explicit unit bookkeeping and finite-resolution assumptions.

Before observation, there is only interference‚Äîa ghost of relations. With realization, a single fact becomes irreversible.
