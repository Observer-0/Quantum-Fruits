# Paper Status And Claim Levels

Repository-level classification for `assets/papers/*.tex`.

Goal: lower rhetorical claim level, mark document type clearly, and separate working theory drafts from analogy/essay material without deleting ideas.

## Claim-Level Guidelines

- `Working reference`: notation/formula summaries and internal registries. Not a proof or empirical result by themselves.
- `Exploratory theory draft`: proposed equations/derivations/interpretations under active development; requires consistency checks and testing.
- `Phenomenology draft`: testable quantitative proposals, but not yet established without benchmark fits and uncertainty analysis.
- `Conceptual essay / position paper`: interpretive or argumentative synthesis; scientific claims need separate technical support.
- `Analogy note / concept sketch`: heuristic or intuition-building text; not a validated derivation.
