### 1. Vision & Purpose

This module transcends static cosmological models. It implements the **Zander-Cycle**, treating the universe as a thermodynamic information engine. It aims to restore the "lost smile" of observers by visualizing the inherent beauty and logical simplicity of the cosmic dance.

### 2. Core Logic: The Information Phase Shift

Instead of a linear expansion, **TSF** simulates a feedback loop based on the fundamental spacetime quantum $\sigma_P$:

* **Entropy Inversion:** 
Entropy is modeled as the dilution of information over the system's spacetime volume.
* **The Hubble Tension Solution:**
The model treats the tension not as a measurement error, but as a **Phase Transition**. The simulation toggles between the "High-Density Coupling" (Early Universe) and the "Low-Density Expansion" (Current Era), showing how the information flow speed (intrinsic $c$) remains constant while the grid density changes.

### 3. Implementation Parameters (The Physics Code)

* **Lower Bound (The Sandbox):** 
The absolute floor for all calculations. No singularity can form; the simulation self-regularizes at $\sigma_P$.
* **The Planck-Force Pivot:** 
When clusters reach this force-density (Quasars/Black Hole Cores), the simulation triggers a repulsion-feedback, preventing collapse and ensuring the "Bounce" (Cyclic Dynamics).
* **Relational Time:**
System-Ticks are no longer global. The `Tick-Rate` is locally coupled to the entropy density. In high-action cores, the simulation slows down, visualizing time dilation as a conservation of information.

### 4. Visual Philosophy: "Look at the Stars"

* **The Action Core:** Black holes must be rendered as **Energy Hubs**. Use the Andromeda-Core as the visual reference: a radiant, quasi-stellar powerhouse operating at $i_{max}$.
* **Organic Pulsing:** The background geometry of the sandbox should "breathe."
    * *Contraction (Blue-Shift):* Gravity-dominated phase, information condensing.
    * *Expansion (Red-Shift):* Entropy-dominated phase, information diffusing.


* **The Observer Layer:** A dedicated UI-toggle for "Child-Mode" (Simpler analogies/Pixel-view) to bypass human conditioning of "thinking small."
