Topological Feedback

The Universal Resonance Model (URM) describes the universe as a coupled lattice of action cells. Unlike standard field theories, URM includes a Topological Feedback term: the higher the local curvature (density), the more rigid the spacetime grid becomes. This prevents singularities and leads to the formation of stable, self-organizing structures (mass).
