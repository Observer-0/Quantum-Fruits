The Kinematic Analogy

Stator / Field Coil ↔ Pure Action Core:
In an electric motor, the field (stator) generates a torque before any load is attached. The Pure Action Core is such a source: pure action $ \sim \hbar \omega $, maximum spin, strong magnetic field, yet hardly any "load" from mass.

Rotor with Load ↔ Mass Burden / Braking:
If you attach a load to the shaft, speed decreases and heat increases. Accreted mass at the event horizon takes on this role: it brakes the spin, coupling energy into classical gravity, heat, radiation, and eventually star formation.

The Spacetime Quantization ($\sigma_P$):
The Quantization of Spacetime itself ($\sigma_P$) defines the discrete mesh through which this kinematic energy flows. It is the fundamental unit of action-area that prevents singularities.

Why the Page Curve Matters

The Page curve describes how information develops over a black hole's lifetime. A curve that only rises would mean information vanishes. A Page curve with a rise, maximum, and fall is the criteria for a unitary (information-preserving) evolution.

Unitary Restoration:
In our motor model, the "falling" entropy represents the core giving its ordered spin-energy back to the universe as new structure.

Pure Action Core

In the zero-mass limit, the core carries energy almost exclusively as angular momentum. Spin generates a magnetic field and an action-based "attraction" $\hbar$.

Mass Loading & Braking

Growing surrounding masses act as a mechanical load. They couple to the spin and brake it in favor of classical gravity ($G, M, r_s$).
