Who is Roy Kerr?

In 1963, Roy Kerr discovered the mathematical 4-vector for a rotating mass. Before Kerr, we only understood stationary (Schwarzschild) black holes. Since every object in the universe rotates, Kerr's solution is the "real" description of cosmic singularities.

Who is Saul Teukolsky?

In the 1970s, Saul Teukolsky derived the equations that describe how a rotating black hole vibrates. His "Teukolsky Master Equation" allows us to calculate how gravitational and electromagnetic waves (information) interact with the horizon.
